Common XAFS materials at BMM

Click on an element to jump to that list of compounds in BMM's collection.

Compounds marked with ✔ are permanently mounted on the reference wheel.

Compounds marked with Fl were measured in fluorescence.

Edges energies given in black text are accessible at BMM. Those in grey text are not.

The compounds listed as being on the reference wheel were measured on the reference wheel. Look in the IR channel for the transmission signal for those data.

Some L1 data may not be useful due to a small edge step.

As of May 2023, collection of data on these compounds is ongoing. Eventually, everything listed here will have an example data file.
